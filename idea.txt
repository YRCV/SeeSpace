App Name: SeeSpace
Goal: Collect classroom data to show estimated free time periods for students on campus.

Logo Concept:
A pair of eyes looking towards an open door.

Colors:
- Primary: #d64045 (Red)
- Secondary: #2a2b2a (Dark Charcoal)
- Tertiary: #dcdcdd (Light Gray)

Features:
1. Search Bar:
   - Fuzzy match for specific classrooms.
2. Building Exploration Screen (Buildings Tab):
   - List of buildings.
   - Each building shows name and "number of rooms empty".
   - Clicking a building reveals rooms inside:
     - Empty rooms first (alphabetical).
     - Occupied rooms second (alphabetical).
3. Nearby Rooms Screen (Home Tab):
   - Shows free rooms sorted by proximity to the user.
   - Favorites section for quick access.
4. Favorites Screen (Favorites Tab):
   - Saved/starred classrooms for quick status checking.
5. Classroom Detail Screen:
   - Full calendar view showing free/occupied blocks.
   - Up to 2 weeks of data.
   - Simple and clean design.

Navigation Order:
1. Buildings
2. Home
3. Favorites

Framework: React Native (Frontend)
